ASUS P5GC/1333 INTEL

PC PROBLEM/ERROR:
-PC won't boot to BIOS.

-Possible Causes:
  -Faulty RAM (or related to memory)
    -Remove RAM and test it on a suitable test computer.
    
-Faulty PCI cards.
-Panel connectors not connected properly.
-PSU not working properly.
-Missing power supply cables.
  -Check the 24 pin ATX cable and
  -4 pin and 8 pin CPU connector
-The CPU might be dead.
